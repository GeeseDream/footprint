为Docker设置socks5代理¶

  1. 创建文件

touch /etc/systemd/system/docker.service.d/http-proxy.conf

  1. 文件内容

[Service]

Environment="HTTPS_PROXY=socks5://192.168.1.2:7891/"

  1. 重启docker服务

systemctl daemon-reload

systemctl restart docker
